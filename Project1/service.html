• Service
    One of my favorite things to do when afforded the opportunity is to serve the communities I'm part of. In my hometown of Galveston, I've regularly been involved in community service including helping orchestrate a recycling program through which people can have their recyclable goods picked up by volunteers to take to the Galveston Recycling Center I also briefly volunteered my time playing piano for the residents at the Meridian Assisted Living Facility Other events and organizations I've volunteered with include "Edfest," "Relay for Life," and "Iron Man."

    My committment to community service has allowed me to meet amazing and inspirational people. It has also given me the opportunity to work with numerous diverse teams of people from all different backgrounds and walks of life, and has helped me practice skills in leadership, teamwork, and cooperation. Lastly, it has brought me closer to my community and my peers, instilled in me a sense of duty towards helping others when I can, and shown me the value in hard work towards a worthwhile goal.
